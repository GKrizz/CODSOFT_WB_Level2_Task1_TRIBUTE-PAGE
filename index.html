He shaped the future of India

Dr. B.R. Ambedkar

Here is a timeline of Dr. Ambedkar:

  • 1891 - Born in Mhow, Central Provinces, India

  • 1913 - Graduated from Elphinstone College, Bombay

  • 1916 - Matriculated at the University of Bombay

  • 1917 - Awarded Baroda State Scholarship for higher studies abroad

  • 1920 - Enrolled at Columbia University, New York

  • 1923 - Awarded Ph.D. for his thesis "The Problem of the Rupee: Its Origin and Its Solution"

  • 1950 - Drafted the Constitution of India

"Cultivation of mind should be the ultimate aim of human existence."

-- Dr. B.R. Ambedkar
